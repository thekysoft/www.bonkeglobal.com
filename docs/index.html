About

Who we are

Bonkeglobal Stainless Steel Whole House Customization is a high-end customization production enterprise under Hongyusheng Group that integrates R&D, production, sales and service. Bogoqi takes stainless steel decoration engineering as the leader and drives the development of the industry. Now through the introduction of Germany's advanced Industry 4.0 production line, we focus on the production of stainless steel whole-house furniture customization. It also covers steel-covered aluminum wall protection systems, stainless steel decoration projects and other integrated stainless steel overall customization services. Currently a leading provider of stainless steel integrated services.
